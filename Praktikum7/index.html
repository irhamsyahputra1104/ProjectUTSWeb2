    Frequently Asked Questioins

    • What should I do in an emergency?

      If you or your family experience an emergency, immediately seek help from the nearest hospital emergency room or our emergency room. For emergency information, you can also contact the Kuzunoha Emergency Room at +62 857 6766 3715. For non-emergency conditions (eg aches, sore throat and urinary tract infections), you can consider other service options such as: Consult with a specialist doctor at the Polyclinic Contact us: Kuzunoha Hospital Customer Service Consult with doctors at First Level Health Facilities (Community Health Centers and Clinics) Consult your personal doctor

    • What should I prepare if I need hospitalization?

      We understand that the hospitalization process can cause anxiety for some people. We are always committed to providing the best care experience for you and your family. Before starting the inpatient process, there are several processes you need to go through, such as:

      Make an appointment and consultation with a specialist doctor at the polyclinic Get directions from a doctor to be hospitalized and undergo certain actions or procedures Book an inpatient room according to your needs at the emergency room or outpatient admissions department Carry out a COVID-19 antigen swab or PCR examination immediately before entering inpatient care

    • How Can I Get Outpatient Services?

      We hope for your help in carrying out the procedures below while you are in the RSUI environment: Use a mask at all times correctly Keep your social distance at least 2 meters Clean your hands with soap or hand sanitizer Follow our staff's instructions to optimize COVID-19 prevention We need to convey that during the COVID-19 pandemic, our hospital implemented policies and procedures for screening for signs and symptoms of COVID-19 for all staff, patients and patient waiters. We will only allow patients and staff who pass the COVID-19 screening to enter our hospital premises. If during screening at the entrance to the Hospital Building you are declared not to have passed the screening, our staff will direct you to consult and seek treatment first with the nearest doctor.
